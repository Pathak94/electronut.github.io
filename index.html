Who We Are

Electronut Labs is the creation of Mahesh Venkitachalam - programmer, writer, photographer and electronics hacker based in Bengaluru, India. We have a passion for electronics, an eye for design, and are uncompromising about the quality of our products. We are constantly working to bring our innovative ideas in hardware to market.

Need help with a hardware project or product? Drop us an email at info@electronut.in. We offer consulting services on AVR and Nordic nRF BLE - including hardware design, firmware development, prototyping, PCB design/assembly, sourcing and manufacturing. We can help you bring your product to market!
