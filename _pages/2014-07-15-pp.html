Coming in 2015

I am happy to announce the upcoming release of my book Python Playground published by No Starch Press, USA. It's a fun romp through Python, applying the language to a number of interesting projects that include a Boids simulation, an OpenGL based volume renderer, and an autostereogram generator. It also has a few hardware projects that use the Arduino microcontroller platform and the tiny Raspberry Pi computer. I hope you check it out!
